UnitransNow

Redesigning a bus navigation app for UC Davis students, hoping to make public transportation in a college town a bit more of a pleasant experience

Overview

My team took on this project to redesign the UnitransNow app for UC Davis students who need assistance with navigating the bus system run by ASUCD (Associated Students of UC Davis). Our goal was to simplify useful features from the current app while incorporating elements of other resources that students use to navigate the bus system.

Process

We focused on user research because we wanted to understand the pain points that make it difficult for students to know when their bus is arriving or leaving. In sending surveys and doing a competitive analysis, we wanted to get a better idea of which app functions were confusing or not helpful.
